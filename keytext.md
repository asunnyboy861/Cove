# App Store Metadata

## Promotional Text
Private voice rooms and encrypted chat. Your conversations stay yours with end-to-end encryption and zero tracking.

## Description
Cove is a privacy-first voice and chat platform designed for people who value their digital freedom. Unlike mainstream messaging apps, Cove puts your privacy first with end-to-end encryption, zero data tracking, and a beautiful native iOS experience.

PRIVATE VOICE ROOMS
Join or create voice channels with friends, teammates, or communities. Crystal-clear audio powered by LiveKit's SFU architecture means your voice data never touches our servers. No recording, no storage, no compromise.

END-TO-END ENCRYPTION
Direct messages are protected with the Signal Protocol (X3DH + Double Ratchet). Only you and your recipient can read your messages. Not even Cove can access them.

ZERO TRACKING
No analytics. No telemetry. No device fingerprinting. Your IP address is never logged, your contacts are never uploaded, and your data is never sold. Period.

BEAUTIFUL NATIVE EXPERIENCE
Built with SwiftUI for a fast, fluid, and familiar iOS experience. Dark mode, VoiceOver, and Dynamic Type are fully supported.

ORGANIZE YOUR COMMUNITIES
Create or join servers with text and voice channels organized by category. Set topics, manage permissions, and build your community your way.

PRIVACY DASHBOARD
See exactly what data is collected and stored. Export or delete your data anytime. Verify your encryption keys directly in the app.

FLEXIBLE PLANS
Cove is free to use with generous limits. Upgrade to Cove Plus or Cove Pro for enhanced features like larger voice rooms, more storage, screen sharing, and AI-powered tools.
